Tour Spots

Geum Oreum (금오름)

One of the mosy beutiful oreum in Jeju, easy to climb, and able to see unforgettable scene.

Secret Forest (비밀의 숲)

The lush, green countryside and is a perfect place to stop, take a deep breath, and stroll through Jeju’s peaceful forests and fields.

Halla Mountain (한라산)

Halla Mountain is a massive shield volcano which forms the bulk of Jeju Island and is often taken as representing the island itself.

Panpopogu (판포포구)

One of my best places to swim. Rent snorkeling gear, paddle boats, tubes etc, for more enjoy swimming

Hamdeok Beach (함덕해수욕장)

One of the top beaches on Jeju Island. Go before the tides recede... Before 4pm if you want to get in the water. Or go for the sunset.

Seopjikoji (섭지코지)

Seopjikoji is a coastal attraction area on Jeju Island. Take a walk along the coast and enjoy the best scenery

9.81 Park (9.81 파크)

If you like driving or enjoying speeding around a race track with the family, this destination is an excellent option for adults and kids.

DAEYOO LAND (대유ATV수렵사격랜드)

Daeyoo Land is the only shooting experience center in Jeju where hunting, shooting and ATV are gathered.

1100 Altitude (1100 고지)

Go to 1100 Altitude in the middle of Mt. Halla and enjoy the stars and the Milky Way with your own eyes.
